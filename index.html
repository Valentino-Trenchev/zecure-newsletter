For the past year we’ve been working on new processes to generate greater efficiencies. We’re finally at the stage where we can focus on quality and improvements across the board. We’ve already started with email and promotion page updates, so let’s see how far we can go…

Testing emails in Symplify, updating templates based on findings, measuring CTA engagement, analysing CTR and OR data, sharing trends, balancing sentence length, aligning copy, adding images and testing TOV language. These are only some of the zenzational details we’re focusing on. Small tweaks will make the world of difference.

The results so far?

We tracked a 13% boost in the Guts open rate and a 4% increase in the click-through-rate over the last 30 days. The needle moved by 1.6% and 0.1% on Rizk respectively.

There’s still loads of room for improvement, and we understand that we’re not the only variable, but for a trial run - it’s a zpeedy start.

Zcoring big

The World Cup is coming. So, how can we promote this global phenomenon when we can’t mention the event at all or the host country, or virtually any word you’d associate with the tournament?

The design team are ahead of the game. We’ve built a visual language around an arabic style that avoids any direct references while still connecting the dots for our sportsbook players. Templates are being readied to tackle all aspects from master promos to individual matches and even cross sells. We’re months ahead and ready for kick off.

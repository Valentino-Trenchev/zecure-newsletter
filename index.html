For the past year we’ve been working on new processes to generate greater efficiencies. Finally we’re at the stage where we can start to focus on quality and improvements across the board. Take the enhancements on our brand emails as an example...

We’ve indexed a checklist of small tweaks from improving CTA’s to balancing sentence length, updating templates to aligning copy better and testing better TOV language. We’ve started to analyse CTR and OR data and have gathered and discussed our findings. The result?

In 30 days we’ve tracked a boost in the open rate of over 13% and a click-through-rate increase of 4% on Guts emails. The needle’s moved by 1.6% and 0.1% on the Rizk rate respectively. There’s still loads of room for improvement, but for a trial run - it’s a zpeedy start.
